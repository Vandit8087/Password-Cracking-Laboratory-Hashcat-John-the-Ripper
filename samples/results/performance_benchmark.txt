=== CONCLUSION ===
- GPU acceleration provides 10-20x performance improvement for fast hashes
- Memory-hard algorithms (bcrypt, Argon2) reduce GPU advantage significantly  
- Power consumption increases substantially with GPU usage
- Both tools are highly effective for their respective strengths
